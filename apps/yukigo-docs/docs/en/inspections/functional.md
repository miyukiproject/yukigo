# Functional Inspections

| Inspection                        | Meaning
|-----------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------
| `UsesAnonymousVariable`           | is the wildcard pattern `_` used?
| `UsesComposition`                 | is the composition operator used?
| `UsesForComprehension`            | is the functional for/do/list comprehension used?
| `UsesGuards`                      | the function has a guarded body?
| `UsesLambda`                      | is there a lambda in the body?
| `UsesYield`                       | is an expression yielded within a comprehension?
